2017 Fuel Efficiency

Due to the recent gas price surge, more people are interested in Fuel Efficiency and how to reduce costs on fuel. In this interactive visualization, we want to understand Fuel Efficiency and seek the relationships between some factors and the mileages of various automobiles. Those factors include engine cylinders, fuel type and the make of the vehicle.
